# DETAILED BAG AND SECURITY POLICY

## Permitted Items

| Item | Size Limit | Condition | Fast-Track? |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Small purse/clutch | 20×15×5 cm | None | Yes |
| Clear plastic bag | 30×30×15 cm | None | Yes |
| Small backpack | 30×25×10 cm | Security discretion | No |
| Medical bag | Any size | Medical certificate | Yes |
| Baby bag | Any size | Infant present | Yes |
| Flags/banners | Max 1×1.5m | No poles | Yes |
| Mobile phone | N/A | N/A | Yes |
| Power bank | &lt;10,000 mAh | N/A | Yes |
| Sealed water bottle | Max 500ml | Plastic only | Yes |
| Small snacks | N/A | Original sealed packaging | Yes |
| Rain poncho | N/A | Clear or team colors | Yes |

## Prohibited Items (Absolute Ban)

### Security threats
- Weapons of any kind (knives, firearms, tasers)
- Fireworks, flares, smoke bombs
- Explosives or explosive materials
- Chemical sprays (pepper spray, mace)

### Recording equipment
- Professional cameras with detachable lenses
- Tripods, monopods, selfie sticks &gt;30cm
- Drones, UAVs, remote-controlled aircraft
- GoPro and action cameras
- Recording devices (separate from phone)

### Disruptive items
- Laser pointers, high-power flashlights
- Megaphones, air horns, vuvuzelas
- Balloons, beach balls, confetti cannons
- Aerosol cans, spray paint, markers

### Substances
- Alcohol (any kind)
- Glass bottles or containers
- Cans (soda, energy drinks — pour into plastic cup at gate)
- Illegal drugs
- Large quantities of food (&gt;2kg)

### Other
- Helmets, hard hats
- Full-face masks (medical masks OK)
- Political banners, offensive materials
- Animals (except service animals with registration)

## Security Checkpoint Process

### Step 1: Outer Perimeter (50m from gate)
- Ticket QR verification
- Visual bag assessment
- Prohibited item surrender or return to vehicle

### Step 2: X-Ray Screening
- All bags through scanner
- Random body scan with metal detector
- Manual inspection if alarm triggers

### Step 3: Turnstile
- Dynamic QR scan (must be live, not screenshot)
- Photo verification (random checks)
- Final entry

### Estimated Times
| Arrival Time | Queue Time | Total Entry |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| H-3:00 | 5-10 min | 10 min |
| H-2:00 | 15-25 min | 25 min |
| H-1:00 | 30-45 min | 45 min |
| H-0:30 | 45-60 min | May miss kickoff |

*H = kick-off time*

## Recommendations for Fastest Entry

1. **Travel light**: Phone, wallet, keys, small bag only
2. **Use clear bag**: Visible contents, no manual check needed
3. **Arrive early**: H-2:00 or earlier
4. **Download ticket**: Before leaving home
5. **Charge phone**: Dynamic QR needs battery
6. **No prohibited items**: Check list before leaving

## Special Circumstances

### Medical needs
- Insulin, epinephrine: Allowed with prescription label
- Oxygen tanks: Allowed with medical certificate
- Wheelchairs, crutches: Allowed, accessible gate entry

### Religious items
- Prayer mat: Small, foldable allowed
- Tasbih (prayer beads): Allowed
- No large religious banners

### Cultural items
- Traditional drums (darbuka): Must be declared, size check
- Team scarves, flags: Allowed (size limits apply)
