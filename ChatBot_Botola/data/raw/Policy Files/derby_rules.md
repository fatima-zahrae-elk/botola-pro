# DERBY AND RIVALRY MATCH RULES

## WAC vs Raja (Casablanca Classico)

### Overview
- **Biggest match in Morocco**: 80,000+ crowd when at Stade Mohammed V
- **Intense rivalry**: Political, social, and sporting divisions
- **Security level**: Maximum — national police + military

### Ticket Sale Restrictions
- **ID required**: CIN must match ticket holder
- **Address verification**: Casablanca residents prioritized
- **Purchase limit**: 2 tickets per person
- **No away fans**: Strict segregation, no Raja fans in WAC sections and vice versa
- **Sale phases**: 
  1. Club members (48h early access)
  2. Season ticket holders (24h early)
  3. General public

### Stadium Segregation
| Zone | Allocation |
|------|-----------|
| Tribune Nord | WAC fans only |
| Tribune Sud | Raja fans only |
| Virage Est | WAC ultras (Winners) |
| Virage Ouest | Raja ultras (Green Boys) |
| Center/Premium | Mixed, enhanced security |
| VIP | Neutral, separate entrance |

### Entry Procedures
- **Double ID check**: Outer perimeter + turnstile
- **Fan color check**: Wearing opponent colors = denied entry in wrong zone
- **Enhanced bag search**: All bags opened manually
- **No flags from opponent**: Confiscated at gate

### Post-Match
- **Staggered exit**: Winning fans leave first (30 min delay for losing fans)
- **Road closures**: 2 hours post-match
- **Public transport**: Extra security, dedicated buses

### Prohibited Specifically
- Any mention of political figures
- Flags/banners &gt;1m (stricter than normal)
- Drums larger than 30cm diameter
- Smoke bombs (instant arrest)
- Face paint in opponent colors in wrong zone

## FAR vs FUS (Rabat Derby)

### Overview
- **Capital city rivalry**: Military (FAR) vs civilian/university (FUS)
- **Smaller scale**: 25,000-35,000 crowd
- **Political undertones**: Historical tension

### Security
- Standard derby protocol but less intense than Casablanca
- Military police presence (FAR is military team)
- **No special restrictions** on ticket sales

### Stadium Allocation
| Zone | Allocation |
|------|-----------|
| Tribune Nord | FAR fans |
| Tribune Sud | FUS fans |
| Virages | Mixed ultras with buffer zone |

## Other Notable Rivalries

### MAS vs RSB (Fès-Berkane)
- **Regional rivalry**: North-central vs eastern Morocco
- **Standard security**: No special restrictions
- **Travel fans**: Common, good atmosphere

### HUSA vs KACM (Agadir-Marrakech)
- **Southern derby**: Tourist city vs coastal city
- **Friendly rivalry**: Less intense, families attend
- **Combined trip**: Fans often visit both cities

## General Derby Advice

### For first-time attendees
1. **Arrive very early**: H-3:00 minimum
2. **Wear neutral or home colors**: Avoid trouble
3. **Stay in your zone**: Never cross to opponent section
4. **Don't film ultras**: Can provoke confrontation
5. **Follow steward instructions**: Immediately
6. **Leave emotions at gate**: It's a game, not war

### For families
- **Avoid ultras sections**: Virages are intense
- **Family zones**: Tribune Sud usually calmer
- **Leave early**: If score is decided, exit 10 min before end
- **Emergency meeting point**: Agree outside stadium

### For away fans
- **Travel in groups**: Never alone
- **No colors until inside**: Wear jacket over jersey
- **Away section only**: Ask stewards if unsure
- **Post-match**: Wait for police escort or clear streets
