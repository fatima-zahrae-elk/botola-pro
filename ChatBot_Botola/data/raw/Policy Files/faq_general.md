# BOTOLA PRO — GENERAL FAQ

## Ticketing

### How do I buy tickets?
1. Botola Pro app (iOS/Android) — primary method
2. Website: botolapro.com
3. Authorized vendors: Marjane, Aswak Assalam (select matches)
4. **NEVER buy from scalpers** — use official marketplace for resale

### Can I transfer my ticket?
- Yes, via app. Recipient must have verified Botola Pro account.
- Transfer fee: 10 MAD
- New dynamic QR generated instantly
- **Deadline**: Up to 2 hours before kick-off

### Can I get a refund?
- **48+ hours before match**: Full refund minus 20 MAD fee
- **24-48 hours**: 50% refund
- **&lt;24 hours**: No refund, but can resell on marketplace
- **Match cancelled/postponed**: Automatic full refund

### What if I lose my phone?
- Visit ticket office with ID + original purchase confirmation
- Paper backup ticket issued (static QR, one-time use)
- Must be done **before** match day

### Dynamic QR — How it works
- QR refreshes every 30 seconds with new cryptographic hash
- Screenshot/photo **will not work** at turnstile
- If phone battery dies: use paper backup (see above) or charging stations at Gates B, D

## Match Day

### What time should I arrive?
| Transport | Recommended Arrival |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Public transport | H-2:00 |
| Driving/parking | H-3:00 |
| VIP hospitality | H-2:30 |
| First time at stadium | H-2:30 |

*H = kick-off time*

### What if I'm late?
- Entry allowed until 15 minutes after kick-off
- After 15 min: entry at discretion of security, no guaranteed seat
- **Derby/Classico**: Strictly no late entry after kick-off

### Weather policy
- **Light rain**: Match proceeds, ponchos sold at gates (20 MAD)
- **Heavy rain**: Possible delay, check app for updates
- **Lightning**: Automatic 30-minute delay, evacuation to concourse
- **Match abandoned**: Tickets valid for rescheduled date or full refund

## Fan Behavior

### What can I bring to support my team?
- Flags/banners without poles (max 1×1.5m)
- Team jerseys, scarves, hats
- Small drums (must be declared at security)
- **NO**: Flares, smoke bombs, laser pointers, offensive banners

### Photography
- Mobile phone photos: ✅ Allowed
- Small point-and-shoot camera: ⚠️ At security discretion
- Professional camera (detachable lens): ❌ Prohibited
- GoPro/action camera: ❌ Prohibited
- **Broadcast rights**: FRMF holds all media rights

### Chants and songs
- Encouraged! But no political, religious, or discriminatory content
- **Derby matches**: Extra security for segregation

## Children & Families

### Age policies
- **Under 6**: Free with ticket-holding adult (must show ID)
- **6-12**: Child ticket (50% off standard price)
- **12+**: Full price
- **Family zones**: Tribune Sud at most stadiums

### Stroller policy
- Small collapsible strollers: ✅ Allowed
- Large prams: ❌ Must be left at gate (tagged, collected after)

### Baby facilities
- Changing tables: All accessible toilets
- Nursing room: Gate D at Stade Mohammed V
- Baby food warming: Available at medical stations

## Accessibility (General)

### I have a disability — what support is available?
- Companion ticket: Free for personal assistant (apply 7 days ahead)
- Audio description: Available for blind fans (headset rental)
- Sign language interpreter: Available on request (48h notice)
- Service animals: ✅ Allowed (must be registered)

### How to book accessible services?
Email: access@frmf.ma or call +212 537 76 87 00

## Resale & Marketplace

### Official resale rules
- Price cap: Original price + 20% maximum
- Seller verification required (ID, phone, bank account)
- Buyer protection: Escrow until ticket scanned at gate
- **Prohibited**: Bulk resale, bot usage, price gouging

### How to spot fake tickets?
- Only buy through Botola Pro app/website
- Check seller rating (must be &gt;70)
- Verify QR preview shows correct match details
- **Report suspicious listings**: In-app "Report" button
