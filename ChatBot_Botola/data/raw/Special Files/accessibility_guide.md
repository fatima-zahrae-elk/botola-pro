# ACCESSIBILITY GUIDE — ALL STADIUMS

## Booking Accessible Services

### How to Request
1. **Email**: access@frmf.ma (preferred, 7 days notice)
2. **Phone**: +212 537 76 87 02 (9h-17h)
3. **In-app**: "Accessibility Services" section (48h notice minimum)

### Required Information
- Match details (teams, date, stadium)
- Type of accessibility needed (see below)
- Companion requirement (yes/no)
- Contact phone for day-of coordination

### Confirmation
- **Email confirmation**: Within 24 hours
- **SMS reminder**: Day before match
- **Day-of contact**: Stadium accessibility coordinator calls 2 hours before

## Types of Accessibility

### Wheelchair Users
- **Spaces**: 80-120 per stadium (varies by venue)
- **Location**: Level with concourse, no stairs
- **Companion**: Free adjacent seat
- **Parking**: Reserved spaces at accessible gates
- **Toilets**: Every level, every zone

### Visual Impairment
- **Audio description**: Headset rental (free, deposit 200 MAD)
- **Guide dogs**: ✅ Allowed with registration
- **Tactile maps**: Available at information desks
- **Assistance**: Volunteer escort from gate to seat (request ahead)

### Hearing Impairment
- **Sign language interpreter**: Available for pre-match briefing (48h notice)
- **Visual announcements**: LED boards display all announcements
- **Emergency alerts**: Visual + vibration alerts

### Neurodivergent Fans (Autism, ADHD, etc.)
- **Sensory room**: Quiet space with dim lighting, available at:
  - Stade Mohammed V (Gate D)
  - Stade Moulay Abdallah (Gate B)
- **Sensory bags**: Ear defenders, fidget toys, visual schedule
- **Early entry**: Arrive 30 min before gates open to avoid crowds
- **Exit flexibility**: Leave and re-enter once per match

### Mobility Impairment (Not Wheelchair)
- **Seating**: Aisle seats with extra legroom
- **Crutches/walking aids**: Allowed, storage at seat
- **Elevators**: All levels at major stadiums

## Match Day Tips

### Before you go
- **Charge devices**: Hearing aids, communication devices
- **Medications**: Bring extra, stadium medical can store refrigerated items
- **Comfort items**: Cushion, blanket (seats can be hard/cold)

### Arrival
- **Use accessible gate**: Even if farther, avoids stairs/crowds
- **Arrive early**: H-2:30 recommended for orientation
- **Meet coordinator**: They'll be at gate with your name

### During match
- **Toilet timing**: Go before halftime rush
- **Concourse access**: Staff can escort if needed
- **Emergency**: Know nearest medical station

### Departure
- **Stay seated**: Wait 15 min for crowds to clear
- **Priority exit**: Available for wheelchair users
- **Assistance to transport**: Coordinator helps arrange taxi

## Rights and Complaints

### Your rights
- **Equal access**: By law (Moroccan Law 03-03 on disability)
- **No extra charge**: Accessibility services free
- **Dignity**: Staff trained in respectful assistance

### If something goes wrong
1. **Speak to coordinator**: At accessible gate
2. **Call hotline**: +212 537 76 87 02 (during match)
3. **Email complaint**: access@frmf.ma (post-match)
4. **Legal recourse**: CDH (Conseil National des Droits de l'Homme)
