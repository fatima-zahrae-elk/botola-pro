# INTERNATIONAL MATCHES — SPECIAL PROCEDURES

## Morocco National Team (Atlas Lions)

### Home Stadium Rotation
| Match Type | Typical Venue |
|-----------|--------------|
| AFCON qualifiers | Stade Mohammed V (Casablanca) |
| World Cup qualifiers | Stade Moulay Abdallah (Rabat) or Stade Mohammed V |
| Friendlies (major) | Stade de Marrakech or Stade Ibn Batouta |
| Friendlies (minor) | Any Botola Pro stadium |

### Ticket Sales
- **FRMF membership**: Priority access, 50% discount
- **General public**: Lottery for high-demand matches
- **Hospitality packages**: Starting 5,000 MAD
- **Away section**: Allocated per visiting federation request

### Enhanced Security
- **Presidential/royal attendance**: Full road closures, helicopter surveillance
- **Diplomatic protocol**: Embassy coordination for visiting fans
- **Border control**: Enhanced for matches vs Algeria, Egypt, Senegal

### Foreign Fan Guidelines

#### For African visitors
- **Visa**: Check if Morocco requires visa for your country
- **Fan ID**: Register on FRMF website before travel
- **Welcome pack**: Available at airport (maps, Arabic phrases, emergency numbers)

#### For European visitors
- **No visa**: For EU citizens (90 days)
- **Direct flights**: From most major cities to CMN, RAK, TNG
- **Fan zones**: Designated meeting areas in Casablanca/Rabat

#### For all foreign visitors
- **Respect Ramadan**: If match during Ramadan, no eating/drinking in public daylight
- **Dress modestly**: Outside stadium, especially in conservative areas
- **Photography**: Ask before photographing people, avoid military/police
- **Alcohol**: Not sold at stadium, available in hotels/bars for non-Muslims

## CAF Competitions (Club Matches)

### Champions League / Confederation Cup
- **Moroccan clubs**: WAC, Raja, AS FAR, Berkane frequent participants
- **Visiting fans**: Usually small contingent, allocated 5% of stadium
- **Security**: CAF delegate present, enhanced checks

### Special Rules
- **CAF banners**: Must display CAF branding
- **Media**: Accredited journalists only in press box
- **Doping**: Random tests, players must cooperate

## FIFA World Cup Qualifiers

- **Friendly matches**: Used to prepare stadiums

### Ticketing for 2026
