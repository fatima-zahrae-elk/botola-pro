# STADE IBN BATOUTA — COMPLETE FAN GUIDE

## Basic Info
- **Name**: Stade Ibn Batouta
- **City**: Tangier, Malabata
- **Capacity**: 45,000 (all seated)
- **Built**: 2011, Expanded: 2018
- **Owner**: Municipality of Tangier
- **Surface**: Hybrid grass
- **Primary Team**: Ittihad Tanger (IRT)

## Getting There
### TGV (High-Speed Train)
- **Tanger-Ville station**: 10 min taxi to stadium
- **Special match-day service**: Extra trains from Casablanca/Rabat

### From City Center
- **Taxi**: 10-15 min, ~30 MAD
- **Bus**: Lines 5, 12, 18

### From Port (for Spanish fans)
- **Ferry arrivals**: Taxi 20 min
- **Special bus**: Match days from port

## Security & Entry
- Standard FRMF security protocol
- **International matches**: Enhanced border police presence
- **Spanish fans**: Welcome, but no political flags (Western Sahara, etc.)
