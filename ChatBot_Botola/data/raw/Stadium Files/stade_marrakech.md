# STADE DE MARRAKECH — COMPLETE FAN GUIDE

## Basic Info
- **Name**: Stade de Marrakech
- **City**: Marrakech, Sidi Youssef Ben Ali
- **Capacity**: 45,240 (all seated)
- **Built**: 2011
- **Owner**: Municipality of Marrakech
- **Surface**: Natural grass
- **Primary Team**: Kawkab Marrakech (KACM)

## Getting There
### From City Center (Jemaa el-Fna area)
- **Taxi**: 15-20 min, ~50 MAD
- **Bus**: Line 3, 15, 20 (30 min)
- **No tram** in Marrakech

### From Airport (RAK)
- **Taxi**: 25 min, ~100 MAD
- **Shuttle**: Available for international matches

### Parking
- Visitor parking: 1,200 spaces, 50 MAD
- Free street parking: 10 min walk (arrive early)

## Security & Entry
- Standard bag check
- **Tourist advisory**: Beware of unofficial "guides" near stadium
- **Heat warning**: Summer matches (June-September), bring water
