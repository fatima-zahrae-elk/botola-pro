# STADE MOHAMMED V — COMPLETE FAN GUIDE

## Basic Info
- **Name**: Stade Mohammed V
- **City**: Casablanca, Maârif district
- **Capacity**: 67,000 (45,000 seated)
- **Built**: 1955, Renovated: 2000, 2015
- **Owner**: Royal Moroccan Football Federation (FRMF)
- **Surface**: Hybrid grass (Desso GrassMaster)
- **Primary Teams**: Wydad Athletic Club (WAC), Raja Club Athletic (Raja)

## Security & Entry
### Bag Policy
| Type | Size Limit | Allowed? | Notes |
|------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Small purse/clutch | 20×15×5 cm | ✅ Yes | X-ray required |
| Clear plastic bag | 30×30×15 cm | ✅ Yes | Fast-track entry |
| Backpack (small) | 30×25×10 cm | ⚠️ Maybe | Security discretion |
| Backpack (large) | &gt;30×25×10 cm | ❌ NO | Confiscated or denied entry |
| Laptop bag | Any | ❌ NO | No electronics except phone |
| Medical bag | Any | ✅ Yes | With medical certificate |
| Baby bag | Any | ✅ Yes | Only with infant present |

### Prohibited Items (ABSOLUTE)
- Weapons, fireworks, flares, smoke bombs
- Professional cameras (detachable lens, tripod)
- Drones, laser pointers, remote-controlled devices
- Alcohol, glass bottles, cans
- Political banners, offensive materials, flags &gt;1.5m
- Helmets, masks covering full face
- Aerosol cans, spray paint

### Permitted Items
- Mobile phone (obviously)
- Small power bank (&lt;10,000 mAh)
- Sealed plastic water bottle (max 500ml)
- Small snacks in original sealed packaging
- Flags/banners without poles (max 1×1.5m)
- Rain poncho (clear or team colors)

### Security Process
1. **Ticket verification**: QR scan at outer perimeter (50m from gate)
2. **Bag check**: X-ray + manual inspection
3. **Body check**: Metal detector, random pat-down
4. **Final QR scan**: At turnstile (dynamic QR refreshes every 30s)
5. **Seat finding**: Follow color-coded zone signs

**Total time**: 15-45 minutes depending on crowd
