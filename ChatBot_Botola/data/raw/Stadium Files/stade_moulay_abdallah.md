# STADE PRINCE MOULAY ABDELLAH — COMPLETE FAN GUIDE

## Basic Info
- **Name**: Stade Prince Moulay Abdellah
- **City**: Rabat, Quartier Industriel
- **Capacity**: 52,000 (Expanding to 65,000+)
- **Built**: 1983, Renovation: 2023-2025 (Ongoing)
- **Owner**: Moroccan Government (Ministry of Youth and Sports)
- **Surface**: Hybrid grass
- **Primary Team**: AS FAR (Association Sportive des Forces Armées Royales)

## Getting There
### Public Transport
- **Tramway L1**: Stop "Stade Moulay Abdellah" (Special match-day service)
- **Bus**: Lines 11, 21, 35, 44 stop near the stadium perimeter.
- **Train**: Rabat-Agdal station is a 10-minute taxi ride away.

### Parking
- P1: VIP/Press (Gate B) — 300 MAD, pre-booking required.
- P2: General (Gate A) — 50 MAD, limited availability.
- **Recommendation**: Use public transport during high-profile matches due to construction.

## Security & Entry
- **Standard Protocol**: Full body search and X-ray for bags.
- **Dynamic QR**: Required for entry via the Botola Pro app.
- **Prohibited**: Typical FRMF list (flares, glass, professional cameras).
